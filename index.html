FAQ

What is WAGS?

WAGS stands for Wednesday Afternoon Golf Society. It's a friendly group for golf enthusiasts who enjoy a regular game and good company.

How are handicaps calculated?

Handicaps are calculated based on your recent scores, using a system designed to ensure fair play among members of all skill levels. For specific details on the calculation method, please refer to the Handicaps section or contact a committee member.

When and where are games played?

Games are typically played on Wednesday afternoons. The specific courses and tee times are announced in advance. Check the schedule or contact us for the latest information.
